the new propensity for art to be able to be fully contained in digital images (or even to only exist within digital images) means, as the reading mentions, a new development in how art is consumed. i would also add that it creates a lot of problems in "ownership" of art. the artist makes the art, of course, but how does distribution affect that fact? corporations steal art all the time because of the easy accessibiliity of these images. could the design of online modes of art/image sharing somehow ammend this?
